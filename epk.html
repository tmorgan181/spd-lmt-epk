Bio

SPD LMT blends the introspective honesty of midwest emo with the melodic warmth of indie pop and the storytelling heart of folk rock. Influenced by artists like MJ Lenderman and Neil Young, their songs feel intimate, direct, and unguarded.

With a debut EP completed and live demos forthcoming, SPD LMT is focused on building a strong regional presence and connecting through honest performance.

Formed: 2021
Location: St. Louis, MO
Genre: Indie Rock
Status: Active
